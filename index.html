Makoto Shinkai's new film 'Suzume'

It depicts a high school girl and a mysterious young man trying to prevent a series of disasters
across Japan.

Suzume had an advance IMAX screening in Japan on November 7, 2022, and was released nationwide on November 11. The film has grossed $104.9 million worldwide, becoming the fourth-highest-grossing film of 2022 in Japan.
